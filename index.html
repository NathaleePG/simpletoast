Simple Vegan French Toast

Vegan

This recipe is dedicated to those who never want to miss out on the fun breakfasts they’ve eaten as a child growing up but have turned vegan after learning the benefits of consuming a full plant based diet! Cheers to good health and clear skin!

Ingredients:
1 cup of almond milk
2 tbsp of almond butter
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tbsp flax seed meal
4-5 slices of whole grain bread
3 tsp of coconut cane sugar
1 tsp canola oil

Optionals:
Maple syrup
Fruit of your choice on the side
Non dairy yogurt


Things you need:
Measuring spoons and cups
Wide shallow dish
Whisk
Pancake spatula
Non-stick pan

    Instructions:

  1. In a shallow and wide dish, add the almond milk, almond butter, and vanilla extract. Whisk together until fully mixed. Then add the flax seed meal and coconut cane sugar then whisk together.
  2. Preheat your non stick pan at about medium-low heat. Once heated, add the 1/2 tsp of canola oil and spread all over the surface of the pan. Use the other ½ tsp of canola oil for your second batch.
  3. Take one slice of bread a time and dip both sides in the batter mixture. Do not keep the bread in the batter for too long because this can cause the bread to be too soggy and fall apart in the pan. Only coat the bread right before you are about to cook it.
  4. Serve with fruits and berries, maple syrup and non dairy yogurt (I prefer vanilla soy yogurt.
